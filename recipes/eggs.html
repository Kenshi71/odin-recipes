Scrambled Eggs

Perfect eggs for breakfast

This simple recipe for scrambled eggs is perfect for a morning pick me up

Serves 2

Ingredients

  • 6 medium eggs
  • 1 small onion
  • Olive oil
  • 1 pinch of Salt and Pepper

Steps

  1. Break the 6 eggs into a bowl and whisk until smooth
  2. Add olive oil to a pan and heat on low heat
  3. Remove the skin of the onion and chop into small pieces
  4. Add the onions to the heated pan using a spatula to turn the onions and coat them with olive oil
  5. When the onions have turned translucent, add the eggs to the pan
  6. break up the eggs with the spatula and keep stirring until eggs are cooked
  7. serve eggs on a plate, add salt and pepper to taste
